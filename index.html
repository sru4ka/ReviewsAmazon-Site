Frequently Asked Questions

Is ReviewScrap free to use?
Every new user gets 20 free credits (1 credit = 1 review) to try all features. After that, credits are just $0.50 per 100 reviews or $5 per 1,000 reviews. Credits never expire.
Which Amazon sites are supported?
ReviewScrap works on 15+ Amazon domains including amazon.com, amazon.co.uk, amazon.ca, amazon.de, amazon.fr, amazon.it, amazon.es, amazon.co.jp, amazon.com.au, amazon.in, amazon.com.br, amazon.com.mx, and more.
Does it extract review photos?
Yes! ReviewScrap automatically prioritizes reviews with customer photos. It extracts the actual product images (not avatars or icons) and includes them in your CSV exports and as downloadable files.
Will my brand name appear in the reviews?
No. ReviewScrap has a built-in brand name removal feature. Enter the Amazon brand name and it will be automatically stripped from all review text, along with any mentions of "Amazon."
How does the Loox/Okendo export work?
ReviewScrap generates CSV files in the exact format that Loox and Okendo expect for bulk import. Just download the CSV and upload it directly to your review platform — no manual formatting needed.
Is this safe to use?
ReviewScrap runs entirely in your browser. It navigates Amazon pages just like you would and extracts publicly visible review data. No data is sent to external servers unless you opt into AI enhancement.
